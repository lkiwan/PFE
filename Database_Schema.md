# 🏢 Institutional Database Architecture: PFE.0

Your system is now fully integrated into a professional, relational PostgreSQL architecture.

**Database URL**: `postgresql://postgres:123456@localhost:5432/PFE`

---

## 1. Schema: `ref` (Reference Data)

The backbone of the project. Every stock is uniquely identified here.

- **`ref.instruments`**: Maps symbols (e.g., `IAM`) to a unique `instrument_id`.
- **`ref.exchanges`**: Maps IDs to stock exchanges (Casablanca, etc.).
- **`ref.instrument_aliases`**: Links different naming conventions (ITISSALAT AL-MAGHRIB vs IAM) to the same ID.

## 2. Schema: `fd` (Financial Data)

Stores the fundamental accounting variables used for valuation models and the Factor Scoring Engine.

- **`fd.financial_results`**: Stores raw line items: `total_actif`, `ca_pnb`, `resultat_net`, etc.
- **`fd.financial_ratios`**: Stores pre-calculated metrics: `per`, `roe`, `debt_to_equity`, and the final `fundamental_score`.

## 3. Schema: `md` (Market Data)

Stores technical and time-series information.

- **`md.eod_bars`**: Daily Close, Open, High, Low, and Volume. The "Whale Controller" watches this table.
- **`md.news_articles`**: Headlines and URLs. Used for the AI's news sentiment context.
- **`md.order_books`**: Real-time Level 2 bid/ask snapshots.

## 4. Schema: `ai` (AI Predictions & Logic)

The "Memory" schema where the Agno Agent lives and saves its intelligence.

- **`ai.predictions`**: Stores the final AI expert reports.
  - `instrument_id`: Foreign key back to `ref`.
  - `prediction_date`: Exact timestamp of analysis.
  - `predicted_trend`: The final BUY/HOLD/SELL verdict.
  - `confidence_score`: A probability metric (0.0 to 1.0) generated by the AI based on data weights.
  - `timeframe`: The investment horizon (e.g., "3-6 Months").
  - `ai_reasoning`: The full 3-paragraph Markdown report generated by Groq.

---

## 🔄 The Native Autopilot Workflow (`run_autopilot.py`)

1. **ID Mapping**: The script converts `"IAM"` into its native `instrument_id` (e.g. 25).
2. **Memory Retrieval**: It queries `ai.predictions` to see what the AI said about `instrument_id = 25` in the past.
3. **Execution**:
   - Updates `md.eod_bars` and `fd.financial_results` via scrapers.
   - Computes live DCF/Whale math.
   - Asks the AI for its expert opinion based on the **Current State** + **Previous Memory**.
4. **Insertion**: The AI's thoughts are parsed and inserted into the `ai.predictions` table for total auditability.
