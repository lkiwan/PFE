🧠 How the Whale Strategy Works — Plain English

1
Every day, compute the 20-day rolling average volume. This is your baseline for "normal" trading on IAM.
2
Detect whale days: if today's volume is 2.5× or more of that average, a big institutional player (whale) is active.
3
Confirm direction: did the price go UP ≥0.3% that day? → whale is BUYING (accumulation) → we BUY. Did it go DOWN? → whale is SELLING → we SELL.
4
Trend filter: only BUY if price is above the 50-day moving average (confirms we're in an uptrend, not a dead-cat bounce).
5
Exit rules: sell if a whale distribution is detected, OR if the position drops 8.0% (stop-loss), OR gains 25.0% (take-profit).
